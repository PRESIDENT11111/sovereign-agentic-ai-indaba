Rwanda Vision 2050 aims to transform Rwanda into an upper-middle-income country by 2035 and a high-income country by 2050. The strategy focuses on human capital development, innovation, and infrastructure.

Key priorities include:
- Transition to a knowledge-based economy
- Investment in education and digital skills
- Development of sustainable cities
- Strengthening regional and global integration

The government emphasizes technology and innovation as key drivers of economic growth.
